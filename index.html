Call for Papers

The University of Hargeisa is pleased to announce its First International Research Conference. We invite researchers, academics, policymakers, practitioners, and innovators to submit abstracts addressing global and local development issues.

This conference builds upon outcomes from the National Research Conference and expands its reach to global audiences, aiming to promote multidisciplinary collaboration and evidence-based policy development.

Submission Guidelines

  • Abstracts max 300 words
  • Include: Title, Authors, Affiliation(s), Background, Objectives, Methods, Key Findings, Implications
  • Keywords: Up to 5

Submission Deadline: 7th March 2025

Notification of Acceptance: 17th March 2025

Full Paper Deadline: 24th March 2025

Conference Objectives

  • Showcase cutting-edge, multidisciplinary research.
  • Foster international research collaboration and knowledge exchange.
  • Promote evidence-based policy and practice.
  • Strengthen capacity for research-driven decision-making.
  • Highlight local innovations with global relevance.
