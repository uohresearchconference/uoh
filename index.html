Call for Papers

The University of Hargeisa invites scholars, researchers, development partners, practitioners, and students to submit abstracts for the First International Research Conference. This landmark event aims to bring together global and local experts to discuss emerging evidence, transformative solutions, and contextual innovations that address Somaliland’s development challenges.

Conference submissions may include empirical studies, theoretical contributions, policy analyses, systematic reviews, innovative community-based findings, and cross‑disciplinary research aligned with the following thematic areas:

  • Population Health and Social Well‑Being – maternal and child health, NCDs, mental health, health systems strengthening, public health innovations.
  • Sustainable Development and Economic Diversification – trade, entrepreneurship, blue economy, GDP growth, economic resilience.
  • Education, Technology, and Human Capital – digital innovation, AI in education, TVET, higher education quality, human resource development.
  • Governance, Peacebuilding, and State Relations – security, institutional reform, diplomacy, elections, public administration.
  • Climate Change, Agriculture, Livestock, and Environmental Resilience – drought resilience, food systems, natural resource management, green technologies.

Submission Deadline: 15 March 2025

Notification of Acceptance: 1 April 2025

Format: Abstracts should be max 300 words, include background, methods, key findings, and conclusion.
